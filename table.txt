<h1>What is the Advanced Premium Tax Credit and how does it work?</h1>

 
</div>
<div class="whoisthisfor parbase section">
<div class="whoisthisfor">
	<div class="whoisthisfor_title">
		<h4>Who is this for?</h4>
    </div>
    <div class="whoisthisfor_body">

                <div class="inlineImageComponent">
                        
                        <p>Because of the Affordable Care Act, many people who buy their own health insurance can get financial assistance that lowers your costs. This assistance is called a subsidy. There are two kinds of <a href="/index/health-insurance-help/faqs/topics/buying-insurance/eligible-subsidy.html">subsidies</a>: the Advanced Premium Tax Credit and Cost Sharing Reduction.<br>
</p>
<p>The Advanced Premium Tax Credit goes toward your health insurance premium<b>—</b>what you pay each month to maintain your health coverage. You can apply it to our <a adhocenable="false" href="/index/plans/michigan-health-insurance.html">bronze, silver, gold and platinum plans</a>. Here's what else you need to know about the premium tax credit.</p>
